Our Services

How We Care for Your Child

From a first consultation through to ongoing management, our specialists provide thorough, family-centred neurological and developmental care for children of all ages — from neonates to adolescents.

Paediatric Neurology
Consultant-led assessment & ongoing management

Dr Khan sees children for a wide range of neurological concerns — from a first seizure to complex, long-term conditions. Every consultation includes a thorough history, a careful examination, and a clear plan developed together with your family.

Conditions we see

First seizures & epilepsy Refractory epilepsy Migraine & chronic headache Tics, Tourette's & movement disorders Autoimmune & demyelinating disorders Hypotonia & muscle weakness Neurocutaneous syndromes
Telehealth consultations are available for families outside the Perth metro area

Developmental Paediatrics
Assessment & support for development, learning and behaviour

Dr Qamer sees children for a wide range of developmental, learning and behavioural concerns. Assessments are thorough and family-centred, often drawing on structured developmental testing, and lead to a clear plan for support at home, kindergarten or school.

Areas we assess

Autism spectrum assessment ADHD assessment & management Global developmental delay Speech & language delay Learning difficulties Behavioural concerns Genetic & syndromic conditions School readiness
We work alongside speech pathologists, occupational therapists, psychologists and schools to support your child

Telehealth for Regional & Country Families

SQ
Dr. Sheeba Qamer
MD, FRACP, MBBS, DNB
Developmental Paediatrician

After completing her General Paediatric specialty training, Dr Sheeba undertook further training in Developmental and Behavioural Paediatrics. She is fully trained under The Royal Australasian College of Physicians and has a particular interest in autism, ADHD and developmental delay.

NK
Dr. Najm Khan
MBBS, MD, DCH, FRACP
Paediatric Neurologist

Dr Khan is a Paediatric Neurologist who provides consultations for a wide range of neurological conditions, with particular interests in epilepsy, movement disorders, headaches and neuromuscular conditions. He completed his fellowship with the Royal Australasian College of Physicians at Perth Children's Hospital, where he continues to work, ensuring seamless hospital-to-clinic care continuity.
